GhiItalian: The Accent in My Accent

A narrative essay on language, identity, and belonging.

  • Format: Non-fiction / Language memoir
  • Status: In progress (drafts ch. 1–3)
  • Editions: EN • IT • DE

Overview

A journey through accents and migrations, where small misunderstandings turn into revelations. GhiItalian listens to the voice we carry: the rhythm that shifts between worlds and the words that both betray and save us.

Sample

“My accent maps me better than any passport: it tells where I loved, where I learned to apologize, where I stopped translating myself.”
