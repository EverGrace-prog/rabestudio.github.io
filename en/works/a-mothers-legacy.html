A Mother’s Legacy

Memoir · Testimony · Family

Overview

A book of living memory: kitchens, rituals, goodbyes, the small phrases that carried whole days. Not a perfect shrine but a real inheritance — in the hands, in the rhythm of home, in the gestures that teach without speaking.
